Hansel and Gretel were brother and sister.
Their family was very poor.
One day they were left in the forest.
Hansel dropped small white pebbles behind them.
The moon helped them find the path home.
Later they were taken even deeper.
This time birds ate the bread crumbs.
The children wandered among tall trees.
At last they saw a strange cottage.
Its walls looked sweet as cake.
Its windows seemed made of sugar.
An old woman welcomed them inside.
She spoke gently but planned harm.
She locked Hansel in a cage.
She ordered Gretel to cook and clean.
Hansel tricked her by showing a bone.
She thought he was still thin.
One day she told Gretel to check the oven.
Gretel pretended not to understand.
When the witch bent forward, Gretel pushed her in.
The children found jewels in the house.
They escaped through the forest together.
A duck helped them cross the water.
They returned home wiser and braver.
