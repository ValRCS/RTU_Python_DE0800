Jack lived with his poor mother.
Their only cow gave little milk.
One day Jack took the cow to market.
On the road he met a strange man.
The man offered magic beans.
Jack traded the cow for the beans.
His mother was angry and threw them away.
During the night a giant beanstalk grew.
It climbed beyond the clouds.
At dawn Jack climbed the twisting stalk.
Above the clouds stood a giant castle.
Inside he found gold and wonder.
The giant roared with a fearful voice.
Fee fi fo fum echoed through the hall.
Jack hid while the giant ate breakfast.
He saw a hen that laid golden eggs.
On another visit he found a singing harp.
Each time he escaped down the stalk.
At last the giant chased him to the ground.
Jack shouted for an axe.
His mother cut the beanstalk down.
The giant crashed and was seen no more.
Jack used the treasure to help his home.
He never forgot the risk of greedy climbs.
