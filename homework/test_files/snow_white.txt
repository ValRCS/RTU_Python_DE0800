Snow White was born in winter.
Her skin was pale as new snow.
Her lips were red as roses.
Her hair was dark as ebony wood.
She lived with a vain queen.
The queen asked a magic mirror each day.
One day the mirror praised Snow White instead.
Jealousy filled the queen's heart.
Snow White fled into the deep forest.
There she found a small tidy cottage.
It belonged to seven dwarfs.
They welcomed her with kindness and caution.
For a while she lived in peace.
The queen learned she was still alive.
She disguised herself as an old peddler.
She offered a shining poisoned apple.
Snow White took one bite and fell still.
The dwarfs wept beside a glass coffin.
In many versions a prince later passes by.
The spell breaks and Snow White wakes.
The forest seems bright again.
Truth defeats envy in the end.
The queen's power fades with her cruelty.
Snow White begins a calmer life.
